Our History

Trends Education Publisher Limited was founded in 2017 by a passionate Author and children's book enthusiast who wanted to provide children with access to high-quality, diverse literature. Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted publisher of children's books, with a strong reputation for producing award-winning titles.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create and publish high-quality literature that enriches the lives of young readers. We aim to provide literature that inspires, entertains, and educates children, promoting literacy and encouraging a love of reading that will last a lifetime.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading publishing house of children's literature that is recognized for our commitment to quality, diversity, and innovation. We strive to produce books that reflect the unique experiences and perspectives of children from all backgrounds and cultures, providing them with windows into different worlds and ways of thinking.

Our Values

At the core of our work as Trends education publishers limited are the following values:

  • Quality: We are committed to producing books that are well-crafted, engaging, and informative, ensuring that every book we publish is of the highest quality.
  • Diversity: We believe that books should reflect the diverse experiences and perspectives of children from all backgrounds and cultures, and we are committed to promoting diversity and inclusivity in all of our publications.
  • Creativity: We value creativity and innovation in our work, encouraging authors and illustrators to push the boundaries of what is possible in children's literature.
  • Education: We recognize the vital role that books play in promoting literacy and education, and we strive to produce books that help children to learn and grow.
  • Collaboration: We believe that collaboration is essential to the success of our work, and we work closely with authors, illustrators, educators, and child development experts to create books that meet the needs of young readers.
