ABOUT ME

Welcom to Viateur Physiotherapist.

Viateur NSHIMYUMUREMYI- Expert Physiotherapy services in Kigali is a licensed Physiotherapist who graduated from the University of Rwanda, College of Medicine and Health Sciences. He is a member of the Rwanda Physical Therapy Organisation and holds an extensive experience working in various settings such as Orthopedic Specialized Hospitals, rehabilitation centers, and clinics which makes him able to accommodate varied clients’ needs.

Viateur NSHIMYUMUREMYI is trained in Stroke rehabilitation and Therapeutic exercise approach to Orthopedic problems of the extremities from the College of Medicine and Health Sciences, University of Rwanda. He is also trained in Basic and Advanced non –Surgical Club Foot Treatment using Ponseti Method from Centura Health and Sports Medicine by the African Union of Sports Medicine and French Anti-doping Agency.

Clinically, Viateur NSHIMYUMUREMYI empowers his patients to take an active role in their recovery and guides them toward their goals. He believes in an evidence-based approach to the assessment and treatment of certain conditions and in the power of communication to help patients take over their healing process.
